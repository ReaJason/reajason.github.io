Spring Security

简介

认证（authentication）：Authentication is how we verify the identity of who is trying to access a particular resource.

授权（authorization）：Authorization is determining who is allowed to access a particular resource.

认证

SpringSecurity 过滤器链：

  • UsernamePasswordAuthenticationFilter：负责进行登录拦截验证用户身份
  • ExceptionTranslationFilter：负责处理拦截链中的异常
  • FilterSecurityInterceptor：负责进行权限校验
